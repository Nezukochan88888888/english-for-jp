# Lesson Plan: Discussion & Debate
**Level:** Advanced
**Time:** 50 minutes
**Objectives:** Express opinions; agree/disagree politely.

## Materials
* Topic Cards (Custom flashcards or whiteboard)

## Activities
1. **Topic Intro:** "Cats vs Dogs" (Use vocab cards).
2. **Debate:** Split class. Group A loves Cats, Group B loves Dogs.
3. **Presentation:** Each group presents 3 reasons why their animal is better using adjectives (Cute, Faithful, Clean).
