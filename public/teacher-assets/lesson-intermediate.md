# Lesson Plan: Daily Routines
**Level:** Intermediate
**Time:** 50 minutes
**Objectives:** Use simple present tense; name daily routine verbs.

## Materials
* Verb Flashcards (Run, Eat, Sleep, Study...)

## Activities

1. **Intro (5 min):**
   - "What time do you wake up?" Question chain.

2. **Sentence Building (15 min):**
   - Show verb card (e.g., "Eat").
   - Student must make a sentence: "I eat breakfast at 7 AM."
   - Use the 'Example' feature on the flashcard back for support.

3. **Role Play (20 min):**
   - Pairs. Interview a famous person about their day.
   - "Do you play soccer?" "Yes, I play soccer every day."

4. **Review (10 min):**
   - Write 3 sentences about your own day using the target verbs.
