# Lesson Plan: My First 20 Words
**Level:** Beginner
**Time:** 45 minutes
**Objectives:** Recognize 20 basic nouns; pronounce them; write them in lower/upper case.

## Materials
* Flashcard deck "Beginner - 20 Words" (App)
* Worksheet 1 (Matching)
* Alphabet sheet

## Activities

1. **Warm-up (5 min):**
   - Play "The Alphabet Song" (Use Alphabet page audio).
   - Ask students to point to letters as they sing.

2. **Flashcard Drill (15 min):**
   - Connect device to projector/screen.
   - Show flashcards 1-20 (Animals & Food).
   - Teacher plays audio; Students repeat 3 times.

3. **Pair Practice (10 min):**
   - Students in pairs use one tablet/phone (if available) or printed cards.
   - Student A shows image, Student B guesses English word.

4. **Worksheet (10 min):**
   - Distribute Worksheet 1.
   - Students draw lines matching English words to Japanese meanings.

5. **Wrap-up (5 min):**
   - Quickfire quiz. Teacher says Japanese, students shout English.
